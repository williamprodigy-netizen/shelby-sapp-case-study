03The 5 mechanics that actually make it work

1. One keyword, forever

Every reel uses the same word. The audience learns one verb. No fragmentation, no broken automations, no "which keyword was that reel." Operational simplicity at scale is the edge — not a limitation.

2. A live event in the middle

She does not send cold traffic to a static reg page and pray. The masterclass is a human, time-boxed event that does the heavy persuasion before anyone talks to a closer. This is the piece most people skip.

3. Volume is the strategy

Daily posting. Reels are the highest-comment format, so every reel is a fresh mouth to the same funnel. She doesn't need a viral hit; she needs reps.

4. Lifestyle = proof = the offer

The Porsche/Miami content isn't vanity — it's the demonstration of the outcome she sells. The lifestyle IS the proof element. The tip earns the save; the lifestyle earns the desire.

5. Price lives on the call

No price anywhere public. The masterclass sells the outcome; the call reveals the number to a warm, pre-sold human. Removes price-shopping from the funnel entirely.

The trade-off to know

Reviews are mixed and the "hidden price + escalation + scarcity waitlist" stack draws scam complaints. Copy the architecture, not the high-pressure opacity. Gloria's brand wins on trust — keep the structure, lose the bait.

04The segmentation truth (the question that started this)

The wrong question

"If every reel uses the same keyword, how do I segment by which reel they came from inside ManyChat?"

You can't — and Shelby never tries to. One keyword means ManyChat has no idea which reel fired it. She treats the keyword as a trigger, not an attribution tool. Tagging every reel inside ManyChat is effort spent answering a question her funnel doesn't need answered.

Where attribution actually belongs

Two layers, neither one is the keyword

  • Which reel is working → read Instagram's native insights (comments/saves/DMs on the post). The reel tells you itself.
  • Which reel drove an opt-in → put a unique utm_content=RL-slug on the DM link. The slug rides into the reg page; your analytics segment there.
  • ManyChat tags → only worth it if you plan to broadcast back to "people from reel X." Shelby doesn't, because they all want the same thing.
